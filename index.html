Percepto Collective 2023

The proposal is continuing with the work made by Perception Collective: the development of technological interfaces for disabilities bodies to play music. Around the idea of Party Technologies (Technologies de la fête) that are interfaces for the collective creation of electronic music that allow everyone to participate in the party.
